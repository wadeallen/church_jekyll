While joining us on Sunday will be the most effective way to to learn what our worship gatherings are like, a brief description may be helpful.

Our early gathering at 9:15am is more traditional in style with congregational hymns, a choir and an organ. The late gathering at 11:15am usually follows a format similar to the early service, yet is more contemporary in style with music usually lead by our band. Everyone is encouraged to participate in either of our gatherings.
