Mid-Week Bible Study is on the 2nd and 4th Wednesday of each month at 1 PM at Riverside Baptist. Wade will be leading this study. Wednesdays Evenings

Each Wednesday evening, those involved in Wednesday night activities gather at 5:30 PM for a pitch-in meal. Everyone is invited to join. The Handbells will rehearse at 6 PM and the Sanctuary Choir at 7:10 PM. Children’s Choir and Kids Club begins at 6 PM. There will be no childcare provided on Wednesday evenings.

Children’s Church

Children’s Church is during the Late Worship Gathering. Children’s Church is for children in kindergarten through 2nd grade. The children are dismissed before the sermon to the basement classroom for their worship time.

Missional Community Break

Missional Communities at First Baptist Muncie began at the beginning of 2013. The ministry runs from February-May (taking a two-month break in the summer) and from August-November (taking a two-month break in the winter). This month, we begin our winter break. Life group leaders will meet during this time to evaluate and discuss the Spring term plans. Please keep this team in your prayers as they work through December and January to prayerfully consider God’s leading for 2014. If you have not been a part of the Missional Community, let me encourage you to consider being involved in 2014. Missional Communities are for anyone in the church, young and old alike.
