Muncie Mission Walk-a-Mile

Walk-A-Mile In My Shoes fundraiser to benefit Muncie Mission will be Saturday, February 8. The purpose of the walk is to take a step into the struggles facing the homeless. It serves to extend the purpose of the Muncie Mission, which is to provide compassionate rescue services on an intermediate or long-term basis to the poor and needy in East Central Indiana.

The walk will start at the Muncie Fieldhouse and continue on a course to the Muncie Mission at 1725 S. Liberty St. Transportation back to the Fieldhouse will be provided by MITS. Check-in time is 8 a.m.; the opening program will be at 9 a.m.; and the walk will follow. To register visit the website or call Muncie Mission ext. 226.
