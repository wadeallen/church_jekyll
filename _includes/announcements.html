Mid-Week Bible Study

Mid-Week Bible Study is on the 2nd and 4th Wednesday of each month at 1 PM at Riverside Baptist. Wade will be leading this study. Wednesdays Evenings

Each Wednesday evening, those involved in Wednesday night activities gather at 5:30 PM for a pitch-in meal. Everyone is invited to join. The Handbells will rehearse at 6 PM and the Sanctuary Choir at 7:10 PM. Children’s Choir and Kids Club begins at 6 PM. There will be no childcare provided on Wednesday evenings.

Children’s Church

Children's Church is during the Late Worship Gathering. Children's Church is for children in kindergarten through 2nd grade. The children are dismissed before the sermon to the basement classroom for their worship time.

Feed My Sheep Food Donations

The deadline for collection of Feed My Sheep food donations has been extended to Friday, November 22. This will allow the more than 1,000 needy people who attend the Muncie Central community dinner to take home a bag of groceries. If every man, woman, and child who attends our church would bring one staple item (a canned item, a box of rice, etc.,) then our church would be donating enough for five families of four to eat for a week. Place items in the toter outside the lower auditoium.
