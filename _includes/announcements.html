Wednesday Evening Activities

Wednesday evenings are a time for various programs, rehearsals and a shared meal. Prayer Shawl (4:30), Meal (5:30), Children Program (6:00), Handbells, Bible Study, other special meetings (6:30) and Choir Rehearsal (7:40).

Missional Communities

A pilot missional community will begin this month. The first large group Missional Community meeting will be February 6, 6:00 PM (dinner at 5:30 PM) in the lower auditorium. Participants have signed up for smaller groups, called life groups and will meet the second and fourth weeks of the month. On the third week of each month, a mission project will be organized for all participants of the missional community. Everyone is invited to join the group on the first and third week of each month. Monthly mission projects will be advertised in the bulletin.

Welcome Soozi Whitten Ford

We welcome Soozi Whitten Ford in worship today. Her special interest in the area of stewardship makes her preaching particularly relevant at this time. Ford serves as the Executive Minister for American Baptist Churches of Indiana and Kentucky and, along with gifted pastors and leaders, is discerning God’s call to ministry in the challenging days of the early 21st century.
