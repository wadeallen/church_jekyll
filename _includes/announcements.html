Mid-Week Bible Study

Mid-Week Bible Study is on the 2nd and 4th Wednesday of each month at 1 PM at Riverside Baptist. Wade will be leading this study. Wednesdays Evenings

Each Wednesday evening, those involved in Wednesday night activities gather at 5:30 PM for a pitch-in meal. Everyone is invited to join in. The Handbells will rehearse at 6 PM and the Sanctuary Choir at 7:10 PM. Children’s Choir and Kids Club begins at 6 PM. There will be no childcare provided on Wednesday evenings.

Children’s Church

Children's Church is during the Late Worship Gathering. Children's Church is for children in kindergarten through 2nd grade. The children are dismissed before the sermon to the basement classroom for their worship time.
