Missional Communities

New communities are forming and will begin to meet in February. We will call these communities Missional Communities. Groups of 25-50 will meet twice a month for fellowship and mission; smaller groups (called Life Groups) will meet twice a month for discipleship and community. We will gather for an informational meeting on Wednesday, January 9 to discuss these communities. If you are looking for an opportunity to get more involved, this may be exactly what you need.
