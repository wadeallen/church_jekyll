Welcome Alistair Brown

Dr. Alistair Brown is an ordained Baptist pastor from the UK who has served as President of Northern Seminary since 2008. Originally from Edinburgh, Dr. Alistair Brown studied at the University of Edinburgh and earned a Bachelor of Arts degree with merit and a Bachelor of Divinity degree with Honors. He went on to earn a PhD with a dissertation examining metaphors of baptism in the Pauline literature. Dr. Brown also earned an MBA from The Open University. Dr. Brown has served as pastor, General Director of the Baptist Missionary Society and now President of Northern Seminary. Alistair is married to Alison, and they have four adult children and two grandchildren.
