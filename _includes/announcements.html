Welcome Betsy

Betsy Whaley is preaching today. Betsy is the Vice President Of Programs And Community Collaboration at the Julian Center in Indianapolis. Betsy is responsible for the programs and services provided to survivors of domestic violence victims and their children. Betsy has a Master of Divinity from Christian Theological Seminary in Indianapolis and started with The Julian Center as a psychotherapist in the counseling center in 1995. Betsy and her husband David have been a part of the First Baptist family since 1994.
