Missional Community

Harvest Dinner, Fall Ministry Celebration and Hanging of the Greens

There will be a combined service on Sunday, November 24 along with an all-church pitch-in lunch following the service. Everyone will attend Sunday school during the early hour and worship together during the second hour. Please bring a dish to share and join us for a time of fellowship and sharing. During the lunch, there will be a brief time for church business to celebrate all that God is doing at First Baptist. Following the Harvest Dinner, everyone is invited to spend a few minutes decorating the church. There will be brief singing and scripture reading, and then everyone will work together to finish the decorating. It is wonderful if responsible children can be part of this tradition as well.

Feed My Sheep Food Donations

The deadline for collection of Feed My Sheep food donations has been extended to Friday, November 22. This will allow the more than 1,000 needy people who attend the Muncie Central community dinner to take home a bag of groceries. If every man, woman, and child who attends our church would bring one staple item (a canned item, a box of rice, etc.,) then our church would be donating enough for five families of four to eat for a week. Place items in the toter outside the lower auditorium.
