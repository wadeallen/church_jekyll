Poverty Simulation

Because our church focuses on ministry to those in poverty, you may want to add an experience to your tool belt to help you better understand what people in poverty face. A Poverty Simulation will be hosted next door at the YWCA on Tuesday, June 24 from 4-6:15 PM. You will be assigned an identity and asked to try to survive four “weeks” in poverty walking in that person’s shoes. Nearly 100% of the 4,000-plus who have participated would definitely recommend this learning opportunity to a friend. Open to anyone aged 10 to 90. Call or e-mail by this coming Friday, June 20, to reserve a spot. (Must have a reservation to be included.)
