Mid-Week Bible Study

In the fall, we will be holding a Mid-Week Bible study on the 2nd and 4th Wednesday of each month at 1 PM at Riverside Baptist. Wade will be leading this study. We will not have Bible study on Wednesday evenings. The first study begins on Wednesday, September 11.

Everyone is also invited to attend a Bible study at Riverside on the 1st and 3rd Wednesday at 10 AM led by Jan Largent. Life Groups also meet for Bible study in homes during the week.

Wednesdays Evenings

We begin weekly Wednesday evening pitch-in meals on Wednesday, September 4. Each Wednesday evening, we will gather at 5:15 PM to set up (beginning the meal at 5:30 PM). The Handbells and Children’s Choir will rehearse at 6:00 PM and the Sanctuary Choir at 7:10 PM. There will be no childcare provided on Wednesday evenings.

Kids Club will begin in October at 6:30 PM.

Children’s Church

Children's Church is during the Late Worship Gathering. Children's Church is for children in kindergarten through 2nd grade. The children are dismissed before the sermon to the basement classroom for their worship time.

Missional Community this Week

The Missional Community will meet at the church Wednesday, September 4 at 5:15 PM. Everyone in the church is invited to join us for dinner. We will gather for a pitch in meal. Bring your children, your favorite dish and join us for fellowship and discussion.
